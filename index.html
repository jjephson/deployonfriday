About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate web developer and designer with over 8 years of experience creating digital experiences that matter. I specialize in building modern, accessible, and performant web applications that help businesses grow and succeed in the digital landscape.

My expertise spans across front-end and back-end development, with a particular focus on creating user-centered designs that prioritize accessibility and usability. I believe that great web experiences should be available to everyone, regardless of their abilities or the devices they use.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing knowledge with the developer community. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with teams that share my passion for quality and innovation.
